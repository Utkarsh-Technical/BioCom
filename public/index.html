About Us

The interplay of Biology and Technology builds our world, and we can harness it to shift humanity from

How is BioCom Different from others?

BioCom is a Biology community, which aims at creating a steady platform for all the Bio-enthusiasts and the technology geeks, wherein they would work together to put forward their ideas, work on research projects, learn about all the latest discoveries related to Bio-tech!

How is BioCom Different from others?

In BioCom, we deal with different areas of Life Sciences and Technical domains to bring the innovation. BioCom encourages peer to peer teaching that makes a better understanding of Science among students. We aim to explore various fields of Sciences through involvement of biology & tech. Our other features include conduction of events like workshops, sessions, online quizes, campaigns and so on.

We Work On!

A diversed and key field of Science that deals with the current trend of biology and implements the latest technology to generate products beneficial for society. Life sciences open a wide range of different spheres and we at biocom work on the various field to solve issues faced by our society.

Oncology & Immunology

This deals with the latest trend in the field of oncology and immunology which enables us to provide all the necessary details to cure cancer by using the knowledge of immunology.

System Biology

The ability to design predictive, multiscale models enables our scientists to discover new biomarkers for disease, stratify patients based on unique genetic profiles, and target drugs and other treatments.

Human Physiology

It is the study of how the human body works. It describes the chemistry and physics behind basic body functions, from how molecules behave in cells to how systems of organs work together.

Psychology

Psychology is science, helps us to understand the universal development of mental functions and social behaviour revolving around an individual or interpersonal relations with a living or non living species.

Prosthetics

In medicine, a prosthesis or prosthetic implant is an artificial device that replaces a missing body part. Prosthetics are intended to restore the normal functions of the missing body part.
